Refund Policy

At ArToe, we are committed to providing our customers with a great shopping experience. However, we understand that sometimes issues may arise. Below is our Refund Policy to help guide you through the process if you're not completely satisfied with your purchase.


Custom Orders

 -----------------------------
Due to the nature of custom products, we cannot accept returns for personalized or custom-made items unless there is a defect or damage in the product. Please ensure you carefully check your design preferences and order details before submitting your order.


Damaged or Defective Items

 --------------------------------------
If your product arrives damaged or defective, please contact us immediately. We require that you send a photograph of the damaged item(s) and the packaging to artoe.group@gmail.com within 48 hours of receiving your order. If we confirm the item is defective or damaged, we will offer you a replacement or a full refund based on your preference.


Non-Returnable Products

 ----------------------------------------
Please note that personalized or custom-made items are non-returnable and non-refundable. This is because these products are made to your specific preferences and are unique to you.


How to Request a Refund

 --------------------------------------
To request a refund, you must email us at artoe.group@gmail.com with your order number, the reason for the return, and photos of the damaged or defective item if applicable. Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of receiving the product.
